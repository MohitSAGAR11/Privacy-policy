Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

XPerience Gaming ("we," "our," or "us") operates the XPerience Gaming mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Credentials for Google Sign-In authentication
  • User Profile: Preferences, booking history, and account settings

1.2 Location Information

We collect your device's location data to:

  • Find gaming cafes near your current location
  • Provide accurate distance calculations
  • Improve location-based search results

Location data is only collected when you grant permission and is used solely for the purposes stated above. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

1.3 Camera and Media

We request access to your device's camera to allow you to:

  • Upload profile pictures
  • Add photos to reviews

Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to upload a photo. We do not access your camera or photo library without your explicit action.

1.4 Booking and Transaction Data

When you make a booking, we collect:

  • Booking details (date, time, gaming station type)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Cashfree payment gateway)
  • Transaction history

1.5 Device Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our gaming cafe booking service
  • Processing your bookings and payments
  • Sending booking confirmations and updates via push notifications
  • Improving our App's functionality and user experience
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), which are provided by Google and comply with industry-standard security practices. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Payment information is processed securely through Cashfree payment gateway and is not stored on our servers. We only store transaction references and booking confirmations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Firebase, Cashfree) that help us operate our App
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition

5. Push Notifications

We send push notifications to keep you informed about:

  • Booking confirmations and reminders
  • Booking status updates
  • Special offers and promotions (you can opt-out)

You can manage notification preferences in your device settings or within the App.

6. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and cloud messaging
  • Google Sign-In: For account authentication
  • Cashfree: For secure payment processing
  • Google Maps: For location services and maps

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Cashfree Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Revoke location and camera permissions
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
